The 3D Chirp Group

The system was developed by a multidisciplinary team, including researchers from:

  • University of Southampton (UoS)
  • SAND Geophysics (SAND)
  • University of Ulster (UoU)
  • Kongsberg GeoAcoustics (GeoA)
  • The Centre for Ultrasonics and Underwater Acoustics at
    the Insitute for Sounds and Vibration Research (ISVR)
